Physical therapy (PT) is a skilled intervention intended to help restore function, improve movement, or relieve pain. Physical therapy can be requested by individuals who seek care for themselves. Physical therapy can also be recommended by a physician, coach, chiropractor or personal trainer. Physical therapy can work on its own to help you achieve your functional goals or part of a team approach with other clinicians/coaches to get you back to living and performing better.
